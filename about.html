About

My bio

My introduction into the world of skin care education began in 2009 at Miller-Motte College. My focus has been centered on body waxing, skin care rejuvenation, including chemical peels and dermaplaning. My passion for skincare came from a life long struggle with imperfect skin and a desire to feel beautiful.

My goal is to share the desire for self love with everyone who has been struggling with skin imperfections. 5 years ago we opened the doors and we have maintained a loyal clientele based on exceptional reviews and word of mouth.

Body waxing is our primary service, going forward our focus is on skin care using only the highest rated products available. In the salon we welcome anyone who needs a warm and judgment free space. This is where the individual can openly discuss anything that concerns them. When the client leaves our mission is for them to feel optimistic and rejuvenated in their skin care goals.

My mission statement

At Wax Queen and Skin we provide an all inclusive and uniqly tailored experience for all women. We strive to provide a comfortable and safe space for each individual and allow them to feel heard and respected. We are not a one size fits all salon. We focus on the individual and their unique skin care needs. At the end of each session, our mission for the woman to leave feeling like the goddes she trully is.
